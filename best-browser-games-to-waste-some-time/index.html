Best Browser Games To Waste Some Time


There are several reasons why the best online games are extremely popular. Unlike the best PC games, which are often more expensive and require a large part of your time to complete, online games are generally less expensive and do not require much time to enjoy. They can take a break from work or school, and they're great when you're just trying to kill a little time or look for a little distraction.

Even if they don't have the strength or depth of a GTA V, the best online games still do what they're supposed to do - show you a good time. There's also something for everyone, from puzzles like Samorost and senseless titles like Robot Unicorn Attack to MMOs like the hugely popular Runescape.

We put our gaming expertise to good use and put together all of the best online games that 2020 has to offer. Whether you wait at the station for your train to work or try to relax in bed at the end of the day, with these games, you will never be bored again.

GeoGuessr

Using nothing more than photographs from Googles street view, players are given pictures of real locations around the world, then asked to identify those locations using only the clues inherent to the picture. Signs, architecture, cars, numerals, and anything else can be used to facilitate guesses, though a bit of worldly knowledge helps more than anything.

Fancy Pants Adventure

In a simple Super Mario Bros. style side scrolling game, you'll have to lead a stick figure with flashy pants in a jumping adventure that requires quick reflexes and some serious platforming skills. Collect flourishes through endless open levels and kiss your productivity goodbye.

Tribal Wars

Tribal Wars requires making long-term decisions and choosing allies carefully. Decisions made will often show results many days later and to conquer the world you'll need to make smart allegiances to create powerful tribes.

Transformice

It's a mob simulator, where every mouse only looks out for themselves. There's no time for a coordinated approach, even worse - getting to the cheese often involves doing so at the expense of others.

Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup

A roguelike game based on Linleys Dungeon Crawl where you must create a character to battle, sneak, cast, and survive while hunting for the Runes of Zot; the randomly generated maps provide countless hours of entertainment that challenges your play style at every opportunity. Or, play Nethack if you like your crawlers classic.
